About Our Accounting Programs

Our accounting programs provide comprehensive training in financial and managerial accounting principles. These programs are designed to prepare students for careers in accounting, finance, and business analysis.

Financial Accounting Fundamentals

6 weeks Beginner In-person

Learn the basics of financial accounting, including journal entries, financial statements, and accounting principles. This program provides a solid foundation for understanding business finances.

What You'll Learn:

  • Accounting equation and double-entry bookkeeping
  • Journal entries and ledger accounts
  • Financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
  • Accounting principles and standards
  • Accounts receivable and payable
  • Inventory accounting
  • Depreciation and asset accounting

Managerial Accounting

7 weeks Intermediate Hybrid

Explore cost accounting, budgeting, and financial decision-making for managers. Includes case studies and real-world applications to help you understand how accounting information is used in business decisions.

What You'll Learn:

  • Cost concepts and classifications
  • Job order and process costing
  • Cost-volume-profit analysis
  • Budgeting and variance analysis
  • Performance measurement
  • Relevant costs for decision making
  • Capital budgeting
Register Now

Financial Analysis & Reporting

8 weeks Advanced In-person

Advanced program on financial statement analysis, ratio analysis, and financial reporting standards. Perfect for students preparing for professional accounting certifications.

What You'll Learn:

  • Financial statement analysis techniques
  • Ratio analysis and interpretation
  • Cash flow analysis
  • International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS)
  • Consolidated financial statements
  • Audit and assurance basics
  • Professional ethics in accounting
