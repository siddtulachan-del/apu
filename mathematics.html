About Our Mathematics Programs

Our mathematics programs are designed to strengthen your mathematical foundation and develop problem-solving skills. From basic calculus to advanced linear algebra, we offer comprehensive programs that cater to students at all levels.

Statistics & Probability

7 weeks Intermediate In-person

Deep dive into statistical analysis, probability theory, and their applications in real-world scenarios. This program includes hands-on projects using statistical software.

What You'll Learn:

  • Descriptive statistics and data visualization
  • Probability distributions
  • Hypothesis testing
  • Confidence intervals
  • Regression analysis
  • Statistical software (R, SPSS)
  • Real-world applications
Register Now

Linear Algebra Workshop

5 weeks Intermediate to Advanced Hybrid

Comprehensive workshop on linear algebra concepts including vectors, matrices, eigenvalues, and their applications in computer science and data analysis.

What You'll Learn:

  • Vector spaces and subspaces
  • Matrix operations and properties
  • Systems of linear equations
  • Eigenvalues and eigenvectors
  • Diagonalization
  • Applications in machine learning
  • Geometric interpretations
Register Now

Calculus Mastery

8 weeks Beginner to Intermediate Online

Master differential and integral calculus with practical applications. Perfect for students preparing for advanced mathematics courses or needing to strengthen their calculus foundation.

What You'll Learn:

  • Limits and continuity
  • Derivatives and differentiation rules
  • Applications of derivatives
  • Integration techniques
  • Applications of integrals
  • Sequences and series
  • Multivariable calculus basics
