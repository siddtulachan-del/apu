Linguistics Lecture Notes:

This lecture covers:
- Phonetics: The study of speech sounds.
- Syntax: The rules governing sentence structure.
- Semantics: The study of meaning in language.
- Language Acquisition: How humans acquire and develop language.

Key takeaways:
- Language is a complex system with various interacting components.
- Understanding these components helps us analyze and interpret human communication.
- Further research areas include computational linguistics and sociolinguistics.
