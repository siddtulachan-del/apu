Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

+

You can become a member by filling out the membership form on our Membership page. Student membership is free, while premium and lifetime memberships require payment.

Are the programs free for members?

+

Student members have access to basic programs, while premium and lifetime members have access to all programs, many of which are free or offered at discounted rates.

Can non-members attend events?

+

Yes, non-members can attend most events, though members get priority registration and may receive discounts on event fees.

How often are new programs offered?

+

We offer programs throughout the academic year, with new programs starting every month. Check our Programs page for the latest schedule.
